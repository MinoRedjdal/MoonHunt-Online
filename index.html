    How to Play

    Roles:

    • 🐺 Werewolves: Hunt villagers at night
    • 👥 Villagers: Find and eliminate werewolves
    • 🛡️ Protector: Can save one player per game
    • 🔍 Detective: Can investigate one player each night

    Game Flow:

    1. Night Phase: Werewolves choose a victim, Protector and Detective use their abilities

    2. Day Phase: Players discuss and vote to eliminate a suspected werewolf

    3. Game continues until either werewolves or villagers win
